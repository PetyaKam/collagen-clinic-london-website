Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostic technologies for comprehensive health and skin assessment

InBody Analysis

Bioelectrical impedance analysis measuring body composition, muscle mass, fat distribution, and metabolic parameters. Provides precise assessment of overall health status and body composition changes.

InBody Stadiometer

Precise height measurement system integrated with body composition analysis, providing accurate anthropometric data for comprehensive health evaluation.

InBody Y Scope

Advanced body composition analyser utilising multiple frequencies to assess muscle quality, cellular health, and phase angle measurements for detailed metabolic insights.

InBody Blood Pressure Monitor

Clinical-grade blood pressure measurement system providing accurate cardiovascular health assessment integrated with comprehensive body analysis.

High-Resolution Dermatoscope with IntelliStudio

Professional dermatoscopic imaging system for detailed skin lesion analysis, mole mapping, and early detection of skin abnormalities with digital documentation capabilities.

SYLTON Observ520 Imaging

Multi-spectral facial imaging system utilizing polarized and cross-polarized light to reveal subsurface skin conditions, vascular patterns, and pigmentation irregularities.

Cardiocoach VO2 MAX

Metabolic testing system measuring oxygen consumption and carbon dioxide production to assess cardiovascular fitness, metabolic efficiency, and exercise capacity.

VALD Strength Testing

Objective measurement of muscle strength, power, and functional movement patterns using dynamometry technology for comprehensive musculoskeletal assessment.

Ultrasound Imaging Collagen Age

High-frequency ultrasound technology measuring dermal thickness, collagen density, and skin elasticity to determine biological age of skin tissues.

Spirometry

Pulmonary function testing measuring lung capacity, airflow rates, and breathing patterns to assess respiratory health and function.

Total Body Mole Mapping

Comprehensive photographic documentation of skin lesions across the entire body surface for monitoring changes and early detection of malignancies.

Total Body Surface Area Skin Age

Systematic assessment of skin aging parameters across different body regions to create comprehensive maps of biological skin age distribution.

VISIA Scan for Skin Age

Multi-spectral facial imaging analysing pores, texture, brown spots, red areas, UV damage, and wrinkles to provide objective skin age assessment.

Glycation Skin Stiffness Age

Non-invasive measurement of advanced glycation end products in skin tissues, providing insights into metabolic aging and skin elasticity changes.

QOVES Scan AI Aesthetics Analysis

Artificial intelligence-powered facial analysis identifying aesthetic concerns and providing objective measurements for treatment planning and monitoring.

Treatments

Evidence-based therapeutic interventions for skin rejuvenation and aesthetic enhancement

Candela GentleMax Pro Plus

Advanced dual-wavelength laser system combining Alexandrite and Nd:YAG technologies for treating pigmented lesions, vascular conditions, and unwanted hair with superior safety and efficacy.

Indiba Diathermy

Radiofrequency therapy at 448 kHz promoting cellular regeneration, collagen synthesis, and tissue remodeling for skin tightening and fat reduction through controlled thermal effects.

Lutronic Laser

Precision laser technology for treating age spots, sun damage, and pigmentation disorders through selective photothermolysis with minimal downtime and excellent safety profile.

ULTRAFORMER III

High-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) technology delivering precise thermal energy to deeper skin layers for lifting, tightening, and contouring without surface damage.

TIXEL Rejuvenation

Thermo-mechanical fractional treatment using heated titanium tips to create controlled micro-injuries, stimulating collagen production and skin renewal with minimal downtime.

Genesis Rejuvenation

Non-invasive laser therapy promoting collagen synthesis and improving skin texture through gentle heating of dermal tissues, suitable for all skin types.

COOLVIEW Acne Laser

Targeted laser treatment for active acne utilizing specific wavelengths to reduce bacterial load, decrease sebum production, and promote healing of inflammatory lesions.

Exilis Tightening

Radiofrequency and ultrasound combination therapy delivering controlled thermal energy to tighten skin and improve body contours through collagen remodeling.

Shockwave Therapy

Acoustic wave technology stimulating cellular regeneration, improving circulation, and promoting tissue repair for various dermatological and aesthetic applications.

TixelTox

Combined treatment protocol utilising TIXEL fractional technology with botulinum toxin application for enhanced penetration and improved aesthetic outcomes.

Dermalux Phototherapy

LED light therapy utilising specific wavelengths to promote cellular metabolism, reduce inflammation, and accelerate healing processes for various skin conditions.

SkinPen Microneedling

FDA-approved microneedling device creating controlled micro-injuries to stimulate natural collagen production, improving skin texture, tone, and reducing acne scars.
