What is the Hair Protocol?

The Hair Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address hair loss, thinning, and scalp health concerns. This comprehensive protocol utilises advanced KeraLase technology with drug delivery to stimulate hair growth, improve scalp health, and enhance hair density.

KeraLase Scalp Laser with drug delivery is an advanced laser treatment that stimulates hair follicles and improves scalp circulation for enhanced hair growth. The laser creates optimal conditions for hair follicle activation while the drug delivery system ensures maximum penetration of growth-stimulating compounds directly to the hair follicles.

Dr Zolman's recommended topicals, supplements, and medications work synergistically with the laser treatment to support hair growth from multiple angles. These evidence-based recommendations are personalised to each individual's specific hair loss pattern and underlying causes, ensuring comprehensive treatment of hair loss and scalp health.

Who Can Benefit from the Hair Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various hair and scalp concerns:

Hair Loss

Those experiencing general hair loss, thinning, or reduced hair density

Scalp Conditions

Individuals with scalp conditions that may be affecting hair growth and quality

Hair Quality Issues

Those with poor hair quality, weak hair, or slow hair growth

Preventive Care

Anyone seeking to prevent future hair loss and maintain healthy hair growth
