What is the Hair Protocol?

The Hair Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address hair loss, thinning, and scalp health concerns. This comprehensive protocol utilises advanced KeraLase technology to stimulate hair growth, improve scalp health, and enhance hair density.

This protocol targets hair loss at multiple levels - from the scalp environment to the hair follicles themselves - while improving overall hair health and preventing future hair loss.

How the Hair Protocol Works

The protocol utilises advanced KeraLase technology to address hair loss from multiple angles:

KeraLase Technology

Advanced laser treatment that stimulates hair follicles and improves scalp circulation for enhanced hair growth

Scalp Health Improvement

Addresses underlying scalp conditions that may be contributing to hair loss and poor hair quality

Hair Follicle Stimulation

Directly targets hair follicles to promote new hair growth and improve existing hair quality

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive scalp and hair analysis to identify causes of hair loss and develop personalised treatment plan.

Treatment Sessions

3-6 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart. Each session includes KeraLase treatment and scalp health assessment.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 60-90 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Follow-up Care

Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and maintain results with additional treatments as needed.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in scalp health and circulation may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement in scalp health and initial signs of new hair growth

Weeks 4-8

Significant improvement in hair density, thickness, and overall hair quality

Long-term Benefits

Continued improvement in hair health and growth with maintained results

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid harsh hair treatments and chemical processes for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Maintain a healthy scalp environment with gentle, pH-balanced hair care products
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a balanced diet rich in hair-healthy nutrients
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and tight hairstyles that may stress hair follicles
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent hair treatments during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Use gentle, sulfate-free hair care products for the first week
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments and excessive heat styling
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in hair-healthy nutrients
  • Use prescribed scalp care products as directed by your practitioner
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that may stress hair follicles
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results

Side Effects & Safety

Common Side Effects

Recovery Time

Safety Profile
