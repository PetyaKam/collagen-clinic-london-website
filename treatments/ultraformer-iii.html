What is ULTRAFORMER III?

ULTRAFORMER III represents the latest advancement in High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) technology. This revolutionary non-surgical treatment delivers precise thermal energy to deeper skin layers, stimulating collagen production and achieving remarkable lifting and tightening effects without damaging the surface of your skin.

Unlike traditional surgical procedures, ULTRAFORMER III works from the inside out, targeting the same foundational layer that plastic surgeons address in surgical facelifts, but without incisions, anaesthesia, or downtime.

How ULTRAFORMER III Works

ULTRAFORMER III uses focused ultrasound energy to create precise thermal coagulation points at specific depths within the skin and underlying tissue. This process:

Collagen Stimulation

Triggers natural collagen regeneration and remodelling for long-lasting skin tightening effects

Tissue Lifting

Targets the SMAS layer (superficial muscular aponeurotic system) for true lifting without surgery

Precision Targeting

Multiple depth options (1.5mm, 3.0mm, 4.5mm, 6.0mm, 9.0mm) for customised treatment

Treatment Timeline

Consultation

Comprehensive skin assessment and treatment planning. We evaluate your skin laxity, discuss expectations, and create a personalised treatment protocol.

Treatment Day

Skin is cleansed and ultrasound gel applied. Treatment involves systematic delivery of focused ultrasound energy. Some discomfort is normal as energy reaches deeper layers.

Immediate Results

Some immediate tightening may be visible. Mild redness or swelling may occur but typically resolves within hours.

Progressive Improvement

Collagen regeneration continues for 3-6 months, with optimal results typically seen at 3 months post-treatment.

Post-Treatment Expectations

  • Return to normal activities immediately - no downtime required
  • Mild redness or swelling may occur for a few hours
  • Some patients experience mild tenderness for 1-2 days
  • Apply sunscreen daily and avoid excessive sun exposure
  • Stay well hydrated to support collagen production
  • Maintain good skincare routine with quality products
  • Avoid other energy-based treatments for 4 weeks
  • Results continue improving for 3-6 months
