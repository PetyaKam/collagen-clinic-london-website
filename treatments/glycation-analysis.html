Glycation Skin Stiffness Analysis

Non-invasive measurement of Advanced Glycation End-products (AGEs) for comprehensive skin biological age assessment

Why: Glycation is a key biomarker of skin aging, measuring the rigidity of collagen and elastin fibers
Technology: Non-invasive measurement of Advanced Glycation End-products (AGEs)
Duration: 10-15 minutes for complete analysis
Results: Precise measurement of skin stiffness and biological age indicators
Tracking: Monitors treatment response and skin elasticity improvements

Glycation is a key biomarker of skin aging, where sugar molecules attach to collagen and elastin fibers, causing them to become stiff and brittle. Our non-invasive glycation analysis measures Advanced Glycation End-products (AGEs) in your skin, providing crucial insights into your skin's biological age and structural integrity.

What Glycation Analysis Reveals:

  • Skin Stiffness: Measures the rigidity of collagen and elastin fibers
  • AGE Accumulation: Detects advanced glycation end-products that accelerate aging
  • Structural Integrity: Evaluates the health of your skin's support matrix
  • Biological Age: Provides a more accurate measure of skin aging than chronological age
  • Treatment Response: Tracks how interventions reduce glycation and improve skin elasticity

By measuring glycation, Dr. Zolman can identify the root causes of skin aging and develop targeted interventions to reduce AGE accumulation, restore skin elasticity, and reverse your skin's biological age.
