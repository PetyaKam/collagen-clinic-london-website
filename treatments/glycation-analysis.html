What is Glycation Skin Stiffness Analysis?

Glycation is a key biomarker of skin aging, where sugar molecules attach to collagen and elastin fibers, causing them to become stiff and brittle. Our non-invasive glycation analysis measures Advanced Glycation End-products (AGEs) in your skin, providing crucial insights into your skin's biological age and structural integrity.

By measuring glycation, Dr. Zolman can identify the root causes of skin aging and develop targeted interventions to reduce AGE accumulation, restore skin elasticity, and reverse your skin's biological age.

Understanding Glycation

Glycation is a natural process that affects skin health:

The Process

Mechanism: Sugar molecules attach to collagen and elastin proteins

Result: Proteins become cross-linked, stiff, and less functional

The Impact

Effect: Skin loses elasticity and becomes more rigid

Visible Signs: Wrinkles, sagging, and reduced skin resilience

The Solution

Measurement: Identify current glycation levels

Treatment: Targeted interventions to reduce AGE accumulation

The Analysis Process

Preparation

Skin is prepared and the measurement device is calibrated. The analysis is completely non-invasive and painless.

Measurement

Advanced technology measures skin stiffness and AGE levels at multiple points for comprehensive assessment.

Analysis

Data is processed and compared to age-matched databases to determine your skin's biological age.

Results Review

Detailed glycation analysis report review with Dr. Zolman's team, including personalized treatment recommendations.
