Total Body Surface Area Analysis

Comprehensive mapping of your skin's biological age across your entire body for holistic skin health assessment

Why: Evaluates skin health beyond the face, providing complete body skin assessment
Coverage: Complete body surface mapping including all skin regions
Duration: 30-45 minutes for comprehensive body analysis
Results: Detailed body-wide skin age mapping and treatment prioritization
Holistic: Ensures treatment protocol addresses all body skin concerns

Our comprehensive body surface area analysis provides a complete mapping of your skin's biological age across your entire body. This innovative diagnostic approach goes beyond facial assessment to evaluate skin health, sun damage, and aging patterns across all body regions.

Comprehensive Assessment:

  • Full Body Mapping: Evaluates skin condition across all body surfaces
  • Age Mapping: Identifies areas of accelerated aging and sun damage
  • Pigmentation Analysis: Maps hyperpigmentation and uneven skin tone across the body
  • Texture Evaluation: Assesses skin smoothness and quality in different body regions
  • Treatment Prioritization: Helps identify which areas need the most attention

This comprehensive analysis ensures that your treatment protocol addresses not just facial concerns, but optimizes skin health across your entire body, providing a truly holistic approach to skin rejuvenation.
