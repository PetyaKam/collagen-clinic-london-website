What is Total Body Surface Area Analysis?

Our comprehensive body surface area analysis provides a complete mapping of your skin's biological age across your entire body. This innovative diagnostic approach goes beyond facial assessment to evaluate skin health, sun damage, and aging patterns across all body regions.

This comprehensive analysis ensures that your treatment protocol addresses not just facial concerns, but optimizes skin health across your entire body, providing a truly holistic approach to skin rejuvenation.

Comprehensive Assessment

Body Surface Analysis evaluates skin health across your entire body:

Full Body Mapping

Evaluates skin condition across all body surfaces including face, neck, chest, arms, hands, torso, legs, and feet

Age Mapping

Identifies areas of accelerated aging and sun damage to prioritize treatment areas

Pigmentation Analysis

Maps hyperpigmentation and uneven skin tone across the entire body surface

Texture Evaluation

Assesses skin smoothness and quality in different body regions for comprehensive understanding

Treatment Prioritization

Helps identify which areas need the most attention for optimal treatment planning

Progress Tracking

Monitors improvements across all body regions to track comprehensive skin health progress

Body Regions Analyzed

The analysis covers all major body regions:

Upper Body

Areas: Face, neck, décolletage, shoulders, arms, and hands

Focus: Sun exposure areas, visible aging, and cosmetic concerns

Torso

Areas: Chest, abdomen, and back

Focus: Overall skin health, sun damage patterns, and texture assessment

Lower Body

Areas: Legs, feet, and other lower body regions

Focus: Complete skin health evaluation and treatment prioritization

The Analysis Process

Preparation

Comprehensive body photography setup with standardized lighting and positioning for accurate assessment.

Image Capture

Systematic photography of all body regions using advanced imaging technology to capture complete body surface data.

Analysis

Advanced software analyzes all body regions, mapping skin age, damage, and health indicators across your entire body.

Results Review

Comprehensive body skin health report review with Dr. Zolman's team, including prioritized treatment recommendations.
