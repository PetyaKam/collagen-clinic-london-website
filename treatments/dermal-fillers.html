What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are advanced injectable treatments that restore volume, smooth wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. At Collagen Clinic London, we use only the highest quality hyaluronic acid-based fillers and bio-stimulating products to achieve natural-looking results that complement your unique facial anatomy.

Our expert practitioners combine artistic vision with medical precision to create subtle enhancements that restore your skin's youthful appearance whilst maintaining your natural expressions and character.

How Dermal Fillers Work

Dermal fillers work by replenishing lost volume and stimulating your body's natural collagen production. The treatment involves precise injection techniques to:

Volume Restoration

Replace lost facial volume in areas such as cheeks, temples, and under-eyes for a lifted, youthful appearance

Wrinkle Smoothing

Fill and smooth deep lines and folds, including nasolabial folds and marionette lines

Feature Enhancement

Enhance natural features such as lips, jawline, and chin for improved facial harmony and definition

The Treatment Process

Consultation

Comprehensive facial analysis and treatment planning. We discuss your goals, assess your facial anatomy, and create a personalised treatment plan.

Pre-Treatment

Topical anaesthetic is applied for comfort. The treatment area is cleansed and prepared using sterile techniques.

Injection Process

Precise injections using advanced techniques including cannulas where appropriate. Treatment typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on areas treated.

Immediate Aftercare

Gentle massage if required and immediate assessment of results. You can return to normal activities immediately with some precautions.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or massaging treated areas for 24 hours
  • Apply ice packs for 10-15 minutes if swelling occurs
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours
  • Sleep with head elevated for the first night
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications if possible
  • Use gentle skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid extreme temperatures (saunas, very hot baths) for 48 hours
  • Stay hydrated and maintain good skincare routine
