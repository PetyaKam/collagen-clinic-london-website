What is EXION RF Microneedling?

EXION RF Microneedling is an advanced treatment that combines the precision of microneedling with the power of radiofrequency energy. This innovative technology creates controlled micro-injuries in the skin while simultaneously delivering radiofrequency energy deep into the dermis, stimulating intense collagen production and achieving remarkable skin remodeling results.

Unlike traditional microneedling, EXION RF Microneedling delivers thermal energy directly to the deeper layers of the skin through insulated needles, providing more effective collagen stimulation and superior results for skin texture, fine lines, and overall skin quality.

The Treatment Process

Consultation

Comprehensive skin assessment to evaluate skin concerns and treatment goals. Customized treatment plan development with appropriate needle depth selection.

Pre-Treatment

Treatment area is thoroughly cleansed. Topical anaesthetic may be applied for comfort. The appropriate needle depth and radiofrequency settings are selected.

Treatment Session

Radiofrequency microneedling is performed systematically across the treatment area. Treatment typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on area size.

Immediate Aftercare

Soothing serums and cooling treatments are applied. Detailed aftercare instructions provided. Mild redness and swelling may occur.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Apply gentle, fragrance-free moisturizer frequently for the first few days
  • Use broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Keep skin clean and avoid touching treated areas
  • Avoid retinoids and exfoliating products for 1 week post-treatment
  • Use gentle, soap-free cleansers for the first few days
  • Avoid makeup for 24-48 hours if possible
  • Avoid swimming pools, saunas, and excessive heat for 48 hours
  • Stay well hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Results will continue to improve over 2-6 months

What to Expect

Immediate Effects

Mild redness and swelling immediately after treatment, similar to a mild sunburn

Days 1-3

Redness and slight swelling subsides. Skin may feel tight or slightly sensitive

Weeks 2-4

Early improvements in skin texture and tone become visible

Months 2-6

Optimal results become visible as collagen remodeling completes, with continued improvement in texture and fine lines
