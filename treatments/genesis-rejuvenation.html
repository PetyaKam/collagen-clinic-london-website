What is Genesis Rejuvenation?

Genesis Rejuvenation is a revolutionary non-invasive laser treatment that uses advanced Nd:YAG laser technology to gently heat the upper dermis, stimulating collagen production and improving overall skin quality. Often referred to as the "lunchtime laser," Genesis provides excellent results with zero downtime.

This innovative treatment works by promoting the skin's natural healing processes, resulting in a more youthful, radiant complexion without the need for aggressive procedures or recovery time.

How Genesis Works

The Genesis laser uses a 1064nm Nd:YAG wavelength that gently heats the papillary dermis to an optimal temperature, creating a controlled thermal effect that:

Collagen Stimulation

Gentle heating promotes new collagen formation, improving skin firmness and reducing fine lines

Vascular Improvement

Reduces diffuse redness and improves overall skin tone by targeting superficial blood vessels

Pore Refinement

Tightens enlarged pores and improves skin texture through thermal remodelling

Treatment Experience

Pre-Treatment

No special preparation required. Skin is cleansed and protective eyewear provided. The treatment can be performed immediately after consultation.

During Treatment

Gentle warming sensation as the laser passes over the skin. Most patients find the treatment relaxing and comfortable. Treatment typically takes 20-30 minutes.

Immediately After

Skin may appear slightly pink with a healthy glow. This typically subsides within an hour. Makeup can be applied immediately if desired.

Progressive Results

Gradual improvement in skin quality becomes apparent after 2-3 treatments, with optimal results visible after completing the full treatment series.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Return to normal activities immediately - no downtime required
  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily as part of regular skincare routine
  • Use gentle, hydrating skincare products to support skin healing
  • Stay well hydrated to support collagen production
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure for 48 hours post-treatment
  • Continue regular skincare routine without interruption
  • Schedule follow-up treatments as recommended for optimal results
  • Consider combining with other treatments for enhanced benefits
