Wrinkled Skin Protocol

Dr Zolman's Evidence-Based Protocol for treating fine lines, wrinkles, and skin texture concerns

Protocol: Sunekos + TIXEL + Botox
Target: Fine lines, wrinkles, skin texture, expression lines
Duration: 3-4 sessions over 6-8 weeks
Results: Significant reduction in wrinkles and improved skin texture
Downtime: Minimal - return to normal activities immediately

What is the Wrinkled Skin Protocol?

The Wrinkled Skin Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address wrinkles (excluding eye/periocular areas). This comprehensive protocol combines Sunekos HA biostimulator with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort, TIXEL ablative laser with drug delivery, Exilis radiofrequency, and Botox long-acting for maximum wrinkle reduction.

This protocol targets wrinkles at multiple levels - from the surface texture to the underlying muscle activity - while improving overall skin health and preventing future wrinkle formation.

How the Wrinkled Skin Protocol Works

The protocol combines three powerful treatments that work synergistically to address wrinkles from multiple angles:

Sunekos Treatment

Strengthens skin barrier function and stimulates natural collagen production, improving skin elasticity and reducing wrinkle depth

TIXEL Fractional Treatment

Creates controlled micro-injuries to stimulate collagen production and improve skin texture, targeting fine lines and surface irregularities

Botox

Relaxes underlying muscle activity that causes dynamic wrinkles, preventing new wrinkle formation while existing ones improve

Who Can Benefit from the Wrinkled Skin Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various types of wrinkles and skin texture concerns:

Fine Lines

Those with early signs of aging, fine lines around eyes, mouth, and forehead

Expression Lines

Individuals with dynamic wrinkles caused by facial expressions and muscle movement

Skin Texture Issues

Those with rough, uneven skin texture and surface irregularities

Preventive Care

Anyone seeking to prevent future wrinkle formation and maintain youthful skin

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive skin analysis to assess wrinkle type, depth, and contributing factors. Customised treatment plan development.

Treatment Sessions

3-4 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart. Each session includes Sunekos injections, TIXEL treatment, and topical Botox application.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 60-90 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Home Care

Prescribed skincare routine including anti-aging products and sun protection to maintain and enhance results.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in skin texture and hydration may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement in skin texture and reduction in fine lines as collagen production increases

Weeks 4-8

Significant reduction in wrinkles, improved skin texture, and enhanced overall skin quality

Long-term Benefits

Maintained results with continued improvement in skin health and prevention of new wrinkle formation

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid sun exposure and use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Discontinue use of retinoids and other active skincare products 1 week prior
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before treatment
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent cosmetic procedures during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or applying makeup to treated areas for 4-6 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive sun exposure for 24-48 hours
  • Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Use prescribed anti-aging skincare products as directed
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results

Side Effects & Safety

All components of the Wrinkled Skin Protocol have excellent safety profiles when administered by qualified practitioners:

Common Side Effects

Mild redness, swelling, or bruising at injection sites; temporary skin sensitivity from TIXEL treatment

Recovery Time

Minimal downtime - can usually resume normal activities immediately following treatment

Safety Profile

All treatments are FDA-approved with excellent safety records when performed by qualified practitioners
