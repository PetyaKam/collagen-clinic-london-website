What is the Loss of Elasticity Protocol?

The Loss of Elasticity Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address skin elasticity loss and improve skin firmness. This comprehensive protocol combines Sunekos HA biostimulator with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort, Exilis radiofrequency, and Ultraformer in non-fat loss mode for optimal elasticity restoration.

This protocol targets elasticity loss at multiple levels, working to restore skin's natural bounce and firmness through advanced biostimulation and radiofrequency technologies.

Skin Tightening & Elasticity Improvement
