What is VISIA Skin Analysis System?

The VISIA Skin Analysis System is the world's most advanced multi-spectral skin imaging technology, providing comprehensive assessment of your skin's biological age and health. This cutting-edge diagnostic tool captures high-resolution images using multiple wavelengths of light to reveal skin conditions that are invisible to the naked eye.

VISIA provides objective, quantifiable data that allows Dr. Zolman to create a personalized treatment protocol targeting your specific skin concerns. The system tracks your progress over time, measuring improvements in skin health and biological age reversal.

What VISIA Measures

VISIA captures and analyzes multiple aspects of your skin health:

UV Damage

Detects sun damage and pigmentation issues beneath the skin surface that are not yet visible

Wrinkles & Fine Lines

Maps facial wrinkles and analyzes skin texture with precise measurements

Pores & Texture

Evaluates pore size and skin smoothness for comprehensive texture analysis

Pigmentation

Identifies brown spots, melasma, and uneven skin tone across all skin layers

Redness & Vascular

Maps broken capillaries, rosacea, and vascular concerns with precision

Porphyrins

Detects bacteria in pores that can lead to acne and skin inflammation

How VISIA Works

VISIA uses advanced multi-spectral imaging technology to capture your skin at different wavelengths:

Standard Photography

Visible Light: Captures surface-level skin appearance

Purpose: Baseline documentation of current skin condition

UV Photography

Ultraviolet Light: Reveals sun damage and pigmentation beneath the surface

Purpose: Identifies hidden UV damage and future skin concerns

Cross-Polarized Light

Polarized Imaging: Shows surface pigmentation and vascular conditions

Purpose: Detailed analysis of redness, broken capillaries, and surface spots

The Analysis Process

Preparation

Skin is cleansed and prepared. You'll be positioned in the VISIA imaging system for standardized photography.

Image Capture

Multiple high-resolution images are captured using different light wavelengths to reveal all skin layers.

Analysis

Advanced software analyzes your images, measuring 150+ skin biomarkers and comparing to age-matched databases.

Results Review

Comprehensive report review with Dr. Zolman's team, including personalized treatment recommendations based on your unique skin profile.
