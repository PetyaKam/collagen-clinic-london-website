What is Ultraformer HIFU?

Ultraformer HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a revolutionary non-surgical treatment that uses focused ultrasound energy to target the deep structural layers of the skin, specifically the SMAS (Superficial Musculoaponeurotic System) layer that plastic surgeons address in surgical facelifts.

This advanced technology delivers precise thermal energy to create controlled coagulation points at specific depths, stimulating natural collagen production and achieving remarkable lifting and tightening effects without damaging the surface of your skin.

How Ultraformer HIFU Works

The system uses focused ultrasound energy to create precise thermal coagulation points at specific depths within the skin and underlying tissue. This process:

SMAS Layer Targeting

Targets the same foundational layer that plastic surgeons address in surgical facelifts for true lifting results

Collagen Stimulation

Triggers natural collagen regeneration and remodeling for long-lasting skin tightening effects

Precision Technology

Multiple depth options (1.5mm, 3.0mm, 4.5mm, 6.0mm, 9.0mm) for customized treatment

Treatment Process

Pre-Treatment Consultation

Comprehensive assessment of skin laxity and treatment planning. Customized treatment parameters based on individual needs and goals.

Treatment Day

Treatment area is marked and ultrasound gel applied. HIFU energy is delivered in precise lines across the treatment area. Treatment typically takes 30-90 minutes.

Immediate Results

Some immediate lifting effect may be visible, with continued improvement as collagen production increases.

After 1 Month

Visible results become apparent as collagen production increases and skin begins to tighten.

Progressive Improvement

Optimal results develop gradually over 3-6 months as new collagen forms and existing collagen remodels, providing natural-looking lifting.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Apply gentle moisturizer and SPF 30+ daily
  • Avoid excessive heat and sun exposure for 48 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products
  • Stay well hydrated to support healing
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Continue normal skincare routine as tolerated
  • Results will continue to improve over 3-6 months
  • Follow up appointments as recommended
