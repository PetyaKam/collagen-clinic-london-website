What is Lutronic LaseMD Ultra?

The Lutronic LaseMD Ultra is a state-of-the-art fractional thulium laser system that delivers precise, controlled energy to create microscopic treatment zones in the skin. This advanced technology stimulates the skin's natural healing process, promoting collagen production and cellular renewal.

Unlike traditional ablative lasers, LaseMD Ultra provides excellent results with minimal downtime, making it ideal for patients seeking significant skin improvement without extended recovery periods.

How LaseMD Ultra Works

The system uses a 1927nm thulium laser wavelength that is specifically absorbed by water in the skin, creating controlled thermal zones that:

Fractional Resurfacing

Creates microscopic treatment zones whilst preserving surrounding healthy tissue for faster healing

Collagen Stimulation

Triggers natural collagen and elastin production for improved skin texture and firmness

Pigment Reduction

Targets and breaks down excess melanin for significant improvement in pigmentation disorders

Treatment Process

Pre-Treatment Consultation

Comprehensive skin analysis and treatment planning. Pre-treatment skincare regimen may be recommended to optimise results and minimise complications.

Treatment Day

Skin is thoroughly cleansed and topical anaesthetic applied for comfort. The laser treatment typically takes 20-45 minutes depending on the area treated.

Immediate Post-Treatment

Cooling gel and soothing treatments applied. Skin may appear red with a bronzed appearance, particularly in pigmented areas.

Recovery Period

Mild peeling and darkening of pigmented areas occurs over 3-7 days. New, healthier skin emerges as the treated tissue sheds.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Apply gentle, fragrance-free moisturiser frequently for the first week
  • Use broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Allow natural peeling process - do not pick or scrub treated areas
  • Avoid retinoids and exfoliating products for 1 week post-treatment
  • Use gentle, soap-free cleansers for the first few days
  • Stay well hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Avoid swimming pools, saunas, and excessive heat for 48 hours
  • Follow prescribed post-treatment skincare regimen if provided

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the LaseMD Ultra Hurt?

At Collagen Clinic London, the LaseMD Ultra procedure prioritizes your comfort. While some individuals may experience a mild tingling or warmth, it's generally well-tolerated. If you're concerned about discomfort, feel free to discuss your worries with our practitioners. We can recommend options such as a topical anaesthetic before the session or incorporating a cooling device during treatment to optimize your comfort level.

What are the Side Effects of LaseMD Ultra?

The majority of clients experience minimal post-treatment effects. These may include temporary redness or warmth in the treated area, usually resolving within 24 hours. As with any skincare procedure, following the aftercare instructions provided by your practitioner is crucial to reduce the likelihood of any potential side effects.

Is there any Aftercare?

After your treatment at Collagen Clinic London, maintaining the cleanliness of the treated area and avoiding direct sunlight are vital steps for a successful recovery. We strongly advise using a high-quality sunscreen to safeguard your skin against UV damage. Our practitioners will provide you with personalised aftercare guidance tailored to your specific treatment, ensuring you achieve optimal results and a seamless recovery process.

How Soon do I see Results?

While some clients may notice immediate improvements in skin texture and tone following LaseMD Ultra treatment at Collagen Clinic London, the full rejuvenating impact typically becomes increasingly noticeable in the following weeks. As the treatment triggers the skin's innate healing mechanisms, such as collagen synthesis, results progressively enhance, unveiling healthier and more luminous skin as time unfolds. Trust the process, and embrace the journey to radiant skin!
