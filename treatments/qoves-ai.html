How QOVES AI Works

QOVES AI uses sophisticated facial recognition and analysis technology to evaluate multiple aspects of facial aesthetics:

Facial Symmetry Analysis

Comprehensive assessment of facial symmetry and balance to identify areas for enhancement

Proportional Analysis

Evaluation of facial proportions according to aesthetic principles and golden ratio standards

Feature Assessment

Detailed analysis of individual features including eyes, nose, lips, and jawline
