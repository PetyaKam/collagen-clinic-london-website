Target: Fine lines, wrinkles, skin texture, expression lines

What is the Skin Rejuvenation Protocol?

The Skin Rejuvenation Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address fine lines, wrinkles, and skin texture concerns. The comprehensive protocol combines a few powerful treatments that work synergistically to address skin aging from multiple angles.

Sunekos is an injectable biostimulator that combines amino acids and hyaluronic acid to regenerate the skin's Extracellular Matrix (ECM) from the inside out, stimulating the skin to naturally produce new collagen and elastin. It strengthens the skin barrier function and improves overall skin health, making skin more resilient to aging triggers, injected into the skin with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort.

Tixel Thermo-Mechanical Ablative (TMA) Technology creates controlled micro-injuries to stimulate collagen production and improve skin texture, targeting fine lines and surface irregularities. This advanced fractional treatment is combined with drug delivery for enhanced results and is applied to the full face and eyes.

Botox Long-Acting relaxes underlying muscle activity that causes dynamic wrinkles, preventing new wrinkle formation while existing ones improve. This is applied to 2 treatment areas during each session.

Who Can Benefit from the Skin Rejuvenation Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various types of wrinkles and skin texture concerns:

Fine Lines

Those with early signs of aging, fine lines around eyes, mouth, and forehead

Expression Lines

Individuals with dynamic wrinkles caused by facial expressions and muscle movement

Skin Texture Issues

Those with rough, uneven skin texture and surface irregularities

Preventive Care

Anyone seeking to prevent future wrinkle formation and maintain youthful skin
