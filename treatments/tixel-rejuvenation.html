What is TIXEL?

TIXEL represents a revolutionary advancement in skin rejuvenation technology, utilising thermo-mechanical ablative (TMA) technology. Unlike traditional laser, ultrasound, or radiofrequency treatments, TIXEL works through the direct application of controlled heat to produce remarkable anti-aging and skin improvement effects.

This innovative treatment is truly personalised to your specific skin concerns and lifestyle requirements, allowing for customised downtime that fits your schedule whilst delivering exceptional results.

How TIXEL Works

The TIXEL system uses a precise grid of tiny pyramid-shaped tips that make contact with your skin for just a few thousandths of a second. This brief, controlled contact transfers heat to the skin in a targeted manner, causing:

Skin Tightening

Tightens the dermis, improving lines, wrinkles, sagging, and pore appearance whilst reducing acne scarring

Skin Resurfacing

Regenerates the epidermis, evening skin tone, reducing pigmentation, and creating a natural glow

Enhanced Absorption

Creates microscopic channels for improved penetration of skincare products and treatments

What to Expect

During Treatment

Treatment takes 30-90 minutes depending on the area. The skin is thoroughly cleansed, and anaesthetic cream may be applied for comfort. You'll feel only a mild warming sensation during the procedure.

Immediately After

Some redness and warmth, similar to mild sunburn, which subsides within hours for lighter treatments or up to 24 hours for more intensive treatments.

Days 1-5

Tiny grid marks (microcrusts) may appear, which naturally peel away. These are signs of the skin's renewal process and should not be picked at.

Week 1 Onwards

Results become visible as skin appears smoother, brighter, and more youthful. Continued improvement occurs over the following months as collagen production increases.

Aftercare

  • Apply moisturiser every 3-4 hours for the first few days, then twice daily
  • Makeup can be applied immediately for light treatments, or after 2-3 days for intensive treatments
  • Allow microcrusts to separate naturally - do not pick or scratch
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products
  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily
  • Avoid direct sun exposure for the first week
