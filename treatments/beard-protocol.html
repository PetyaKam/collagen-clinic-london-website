What is the Beard Protocol?

The Beard Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address beard growth, thickness, and facial hair health concerns. This comprehensive protocol utilises advanced KeraLase technology with drug delivery to stimulate beard growth, improve hair density, and enhance facial hair quality.

This protocol targets beard growth at multiple levels - from the hair follicles to the surrounding skin - while improving overall facial hair health and preventing future hair loss.

How the Beard Protocol Works

The protocol utilises advanced KeraLase technology with drug delivery to address beard growth from multiple angles:

KeraLase Laser Treatment

Advanced laser treatment that stimulates hair follicles and improves circulation in the beard area for enhanced hair growth

Drug Delivery System

Enhanced penetration of growth-stimulating compounds directly to hair follicles for maximum efficacy

Hair Follicle Stimulation

Directly targets beard hair follicles to promote new hair growth and improve existing hair quality and thickness

Who Can Benefit from the Beard Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various beard and facial hair concerns:

Patchy Beard Growth

Those with uneven beard growth, patchy areas, or gaps in facial hair coverage

Thin or Sparse Beard

Individuals with thin, sparse beard hair or reduced hair density

Slow Beard Growth

Those experiencing slow or stunted beard growth despite regular shaving or grooming

Beard Quality Issues

Anyone seeking to improve beard thickness, texture, and overall facial hair quality

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive assessment of beard growth patterns, hair density, and facial hair health. Customised treatment plan development.

Treatment Sessions

3-6 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart. Each session includes KeraLase laser treatment with drug delivery.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 60-90 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Home Care

Prescribed topical treatments, supplements, and medications to maintain and enhance results between sessions.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in skin health and circulation in the beard area may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement in beard area health and initial signs of new hair growth

Weeks 4-8

Significant improvement in beard density, thickness, and overall coverage

Long-term Benefits

Continued improvement in beard health and growth with maintained results

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid harsh facial hair treatments and chemical processes for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Maintain a healthy skincare routine in the beard area with gentle, pH-balanced products
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a balanced diet rich in hair-healthy nutrients
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and tight grooming that may stress hair follicles
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent facial hair treatments during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Use gentle, sulfate-free skincare products in the beard area for the first week
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments and excessive heat styling
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in hair-healthy nutrients
  • Use prescribed topical treatments, supplements, and medications as directed by your practitioner
  • Avoid tight grooming that may stress hair follicles
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results

Side Effects & Safety

KeraLase treatment with drug delivery has an excellent safety profile when administered by qualified practitioners:

Common Side Effects

Mild skin sensitivity or redness in the beard area that typically resolves within a few hours

Recovery Time

Minimal downtime - can usually resume normal activities immediately following treatment

Safety Profile

KeraLase treatment is FDA-approved with excellent safety records when performed by qualified practitioners
