What is Botox?

Botox (botulinum toxin type A) is a clinically proven, FDA-approved injectable treatment that temporarily relaxes muscle activity to reduce the appearance of dynamic wrinkles. At Collagen Clinic London, we use premium botulinum toxin products including Alluzience (long-lasting) to deliver natural-looking results that preserve your facial expressions while smoothing fine lines and wrinkles.

Our expert practitioners use precise injection techniques to target specific muscles, ensuring you maintain your natural expressions and character while achieving a refreshed, rejuvenated appearance.

How Botox Works

Botox works by temporarily blocking nerve signals to targeted muscles, preventing them from contracting. This process:

Relaxes Muscles

Reduces muscle activity that causes dynamic wrinkles and expression lines

Prevents Wrinkles

Stops new wrinkles from forming by preventing repetitive muscle movements

Enhances Features

Can be used strategically to lift eyebrows, correct smiles, and improve facial symmetry

The Treatment Process

Consultation

Comprehensive facial analysis and treatment planning. We discuss your goals, assess your facial anatomy and muscle movement, and create a personalised treatment plan.

Pre-Treatment

The treatment area is marked and cleansed using sterile techniques. Topical anaesthetic may be applied for comfort if needed.

Injection Process

Precise injections into targeted muscles using fine needles. Treatment typically takes 10-20 minutes depending on areas treated.

Immediate Aftercare

Post-treatment instructions provided. You can return to normal activities immediately with some precautions. Avoid lying down for 4 hours after treatment.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid lying down or bending over for 4 hours after treatment
  • Do not rub or massage treated areas for 24 hours
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Stay upright and avoid pressure on treated areas
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications if possible
  • Do not apply makeup immediately after treatment
  • Avoid extreme temperatures (saunas, very hot baths) for 24 hours
  • Results typically appear within 3-7 days, with full effects visible in 2 weeks
