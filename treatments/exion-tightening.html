What is EXION RF Tightening?

EXION RF Tightening is an advanced radiofrequency treatment that delivers controlled thermal energy to the deeper layers of the skin, stimulating collagen production and achieving remarkable skin tightening and lifting results without surgery or significant downtime.

This innovative technology uses radiofrequency energy to heat the dermis, triggering the body's natural healing response and promoting the production of new collagen and elastin fibers, resulting in tighter, firmer, and more youthful-looking skin.

How EXION RF Tightening Works

EXION RF Tightening uses advanced radiofrequency technology to deliver controlled thermal energy to the skin:

Collagen Stimulation

Heats the dermis to stimulate natural collagen and elastin production for long-lasting skin tightening

Immediate Contraction

Existing collagen fibers contract immediately, providing instant tightening effects

Progressive Improvement

New collagen formation continues over several months, providing progressive and natural-looking results

The Treatment Process

Consultation

Comprehensive skin assessment to evaluate skin laxity and treatment goals. Customized treatment plan development based on individual needs.

Pre-Treatment

Treatment area is cleansed and prepared. Ultrasound gel may be applied to ensure optimal energy delivery.

Treatment Session

Radiofrequency energy is delivered systematically across the treatment area. Treatment typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on area size.

Immediate Aftercare

Cooling gel may be applied. You can return to normal activities immediately with minimal restrictions.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Apply gentle moisturizer and SPF 30+ daily
  • Avoid excessive heat and sun exposure for 48 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products
  • Stay well hydrated to support healing
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Continue normal skincare routine as tolerated
  • Results will continue to improve over 2-6 months
  • Follow up appointments as recommended

What to Expect

Immediate Results

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement as new collagen production begins

Months 2-6

Optimal results become visible as collagen remodeling completes

Long-Term

Results can last 12-18 months with proper skin care and maintenance
