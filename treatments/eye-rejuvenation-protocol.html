What is the Eye Rejuvenation Protocol?

The Eye Rejuvenation Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address upper and lower eye area concerns. This comprehensive protocol combines Sunekos HA biostimulator with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort, precision laser treatment, and topical botox for enhanced results.

The protocol includes TIXEL laser for upper eye non-surgical blepharoplasty, skin-dependent laser selection (LaseMD or Excel V Coolview), and topical botox long-acting for enhanced collagen formation and pigment removal.

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive eye area analysis to assess concerns and determine the most suitable laser treatment. Customised protocol development.

Treatment Sessions

3-4 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart. Each session includes Sunekos injections and customised laser treatment.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 60-90 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Follow-up Care

Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and maintain results with additional treatments as needed.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in skin texture and hydration may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement in skin texture and reduction in fine lines as collagen production increases

Weeks 4-8

Significant improvement in eye area appearance, reduced fine lines, and enhanced skin quality

Long-term Benefits

Maintained results with continued improvement in eye area health and appearance

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid sun exposure and use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Discontinue use of retinoids and other active skincare products 1 week prior
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before treatment
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent cosmetic procedures during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or applying makeup to treated areas for 4-6 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive sun exposure for 24-48 hours
  • Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Use cool compresses if experiencing mild redness or swelling
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results
