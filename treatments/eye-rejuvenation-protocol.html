Eye Rejuvenation Protocol

Dr Zolman's Evidence-Based Protocol for treating eye area concerns including fine lines, dark circles, and sagging

Protocol Includes: 1 session of Sunekos Biostimulator Full Face & Eyes
1 session of LaseMD or Excel V Coolview Laser (skin-dependent)
1 session of Tixel TMA Technology for upper eye non-surgical blepharoplasty
Target: Upper and lower eye area - fine lines, dark circles, sagging, crow's feet
Sessions Needed: 3 Protocols

What is the Eye Rejuvenation Protocol?

The Eye Rejuvenation Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address eye area concerns including fine lines, dark circles, and sagging. The comprehensive protocol combines a few powerful treatments that work synergistically to address eye area aging from multiple angles.

Sunekos is an injectable biostimulator that combines amino acids and hyaluronic acid to regenerate the skin's Extracellular Matrix (ECM) from the inside out, stimulating the skin to naturally produce new collagen and elastin. It strengthens the skin barrier function and improves overall skin health, making skin more resilient to aging triggers, injected into the skin with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort.

LaseMD or Excel V Coolview Laser (skin-dependent) provides precision laser treatment tailored to individual needs. LaseMD targets pigmentation and dark circles, while Excel V Coolview addresses vascular concerns and overall rejuvenation in the delicate eye area.

Tixel Thermo-Mechanical Ablative (TMA) Technology provides non-surgical blepharoplasty for the upper eye area, creating controlled micro-injuries to stimulate collagen production and provide lifting and tightening effects without downtime.
