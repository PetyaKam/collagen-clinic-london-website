What is the Volume Loss Protocol?

The Volume Loss Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address volume loss in temples, jaw recession, and around the mouth. This comprehensive protocol combines Julaine poly-lactic acid (non-inflammatory sculptra) with the world's best hyaluronic acid filler and associated Botox for stabilisation.

This protocol targets volume loss at multiple levels, providing both immediate and long-term volume restoration with natural-looking results and enhanced stability.
