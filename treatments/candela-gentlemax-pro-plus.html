What is Candela GentleMax Pro Plus?

The Candela GentleMax Pro Plus represents the pinnacle of laser technology, combining two powerful wavelengths - Alexandrite (755nm) and Nd:YAG (1064nm) - in one advanced system. This dual-wavelength capability allows us to safely and effectively treat all skin types and a wide range of conditions.

As the gold standard in laser treatments, the GentleMax Pro Plus delivers superior results for permanent hair reduction, pigmented lesion removal, and vascular condition treatment, all whilst maintaining the highest safety standards.

How GentleMax Pro Plus Works

The system uses selective photothermolysis - precisely targeting specific chromophores (melanin in hair and pigmented lesions, haemoglobin in vascular lesions) whilst protecting surrounding tissue:

Alexandrite 755nm

Ideal for lighter skin types (I-IV), excellent for hair removal and superficial pigmented lesions

Nd:YAG 1064nm

Safe for darker skin types (IV-VI), effective for deeper hair follicles and vascular conditions

Dynamic Cooling Device

Integrated cooling system protects the epidermis and maximises comfort during treatment

Treatment Process

Consultation

Comprehensive skin and hair assessment. We determine your skin type, discuss treatment goals, and create a personalised treatment plan with appropriate wavelength selection.

Pre-Treatment Preparation

Treatment area is cleansed and, if necessary, shaved. Protective eyewear is provided. Cooling gel may be applied for additional comfort.

Laser Treatment

Precise laser pulses are delivered to the treatment area. The integrated cooling system ensures comfort. Treatment duration varies from 15 minutes to 2 hours depending on area size.

Post-Treatment Care

Immediate cooling and soothing gel application. Detailed aftercare instructions provided. Most patients can return to normal activities immediately.

Post-Treatment Expectations

  • Mild redness and swelling may occur for 24-48 hours
  • For hair removal: hair will shed over 2-3 weeks following treatment
  • For pigmented lesions: darkening occurs initially, then peeling over 1-2 weeks
  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily and avoid sun exposure
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for 48 hours
  • Avoid hot baths, saunas, and vigorous exercise for 24 hours
  • Do not wax, pluck, or bleach between hair removal sessions
  • Moisturise treated areas regularly to support healing
