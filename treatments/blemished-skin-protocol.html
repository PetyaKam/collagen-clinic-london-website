What is the Blemished Skin Protocol?

The Blemished Skin Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address problematic skin, blemishes, and skin imperfections. This comprehensive protocol combines Tixel thermo-mechanical ablative (TMA) technology with drug delivery for enhanced penetration and effectiveness, along with Fusion Meso F-ACN - a specialized mesotherapy solution formulated to target blemished skin.

Tixel thermo-mechanical ablative (TMA) technology creates precise micro-channels in the skin, allowing for optimal delivery of active ingredients deep into the dermis while promoting skin renewal and resurfacing. This advanced technology ensures maximum penetration of treatment compounds while minimising downtime and discomfort.

Fusion Meso F-ACN is a specialised mesotherapy solution that combines hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, ZnPCA (Zinc Pyrrolidone Carboxylic Acid), and panthenol. This targeted formulation works to reduce inflammation, control sebum production, and improve skin clarity. When delivered through the Tixel-created micro-channels, these active ingredients reach deeper skin layers for more effective treatment of problematic skin.

Who Can Benefit from the Blemished Skin Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various problematic skin concerns:

Acne-Prone Skin

Those with active acne, breakouts, and recurrent blemishes

Post-Acne Marks

Individuals with residual marks, scars, or pigmentation from previous breakouts

Oily Problematic Skin

Those with excessive sebum production and related skin issues

Inflammatory Skin

Anyone with inflammatory skin conditions and redness associated with blemishes
