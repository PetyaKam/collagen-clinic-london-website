What is the Blemished Skin Protocol?

The Blemished Skin Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address problematic skin, blemishes, and skin imperfections. This comprehensive protocol combines Tixel thermo-mechanical ablative (TMA) technology with drug delivery for enhanced penetration and effectiveness, along with Fusion Meso F-ACN - a specialized mesotherapy solution formulated to target blemished skin.

This protocol targets problematic skin at multiple levels, working to reduce blemishes, improve skin texture, and restore clear, healthy-looking skin through advanced thermo-mechanical ablative technology and targeted mesotherapy.

Treatment Components

Tixel Thermo-Mechanical Ablative (TMA) Technology with Drug Delivery

Thermo-Mechanical Skin Resurfacing

Purpose: Creates controlled micro-channels in the skin for enhanced drug delivery and skin resurfacing

Benefits: Improved penetration of active ingredients, reduced blemishes, enhanced skin texture

Technology: Advanced thermo-mechanical ablative technology with drug delivery system

Fusion Meso F-ACN

Specialized Mesotherapy Solution

Purpose: Targeted treatment for blemished skin using a combination of active ingredients

Benefits: Reduces inflammation, controls sebum production, improves skin clarity

Active Ingredients:

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Hydration and skin barrier support
  • Niacinamide: Anti-inflammatory and sebum regulation
  • ZnPCA (Zinc Pyrrolidone Carboxylic Acid): Antimicrobial and healing properties
  • Panthenol: Soothing and healing enhancement

How the Blemished Skin Protocol Works

The protocol combines two powerful treatments that work synergistically to address problematic skin:

Tixel Thermo-Mechanical Ablative (TMA) Technology Treatment

Creates precise micro-channels in the skin, allowing for optimal delivery of active ingredients deep into the dermis while promoting skin renewal and resurfacing

Fusion Meso F-ACN Delivery

Delivers a specialized combination of hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, ZnPCA, and panthenol directly to problem areas for targeted treatment and improved skin health

Synergistic Effects

The combination enhances penetration, reduces inflammation, controls sebum production, and promotes healing for comprehensive blemish treatment

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive skin analysis to assess skin type, blemish concerns, and contributing factors. Customised treatment plan development.

Treatment Sessions

3-4 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart. Each session includes Tixel thermo-mechanical ablative (TMA) technology treatment with Fusion Meso F-ACN application.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 45-60 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Home Care

Prescribed skincare routine to maintain results and support skin healing between sessions.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in skin texture and appearance may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual reduction in active blemishes and improvement in skin clarity

Weeks 4-8

Significant improvement in skin quality, reduced blemishes, and enhanced skin texture

Long-term Benefits

Maintained results with continued improvement in skin health and reduced propensity for future breakouts

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid sun exposure and use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Discontinue use of retinoids and other active skincare products 1 week prior
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before treatment
  • Ensure skin is clean and free of active infections or open wounds
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent cosmetic procedures during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid sun exposure completely for 48 hours after treatment
  • Use broad-spectrum sunscreen (SPF 50+) daily and reapply every 2 hours when outdoors
  • Keep treated areas clean and follow prescribed skincare routine
  • Avoid picking or touching treated areas
  • Use gentle, non-comedogenic skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid makeup for 24-48 hours after treatment if possible
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results
