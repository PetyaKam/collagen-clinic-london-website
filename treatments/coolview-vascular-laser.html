COOLVIEW Vascular Laser

Advanced dual-wavelength laser system for precise treatment of vascular conditions and facial redness

Why: Precise targeting of vascular conditions with dual wavelength technology for optimal results
Areas: Face, legs, body - treating spider veins, broken capillaries, and redness
Sessions: 1-4 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart
Results: Significant reduction in visible vessels and overall redness
Downtime: Minimal - possible temporary redness for 24-48 hours

What is COOLVIEW Vascular Laser?

The COOLVIEW Vascular Laser is a state-of-the-art dual-wavelength laser system specifically designed for the treatment of vascular conditions. Combining both 532nm KTP and 1064nm Nd:YAG wavelengths, this advanced technology allows for precise targeting of both superficial and deeper vascular lesions.

With integrated cooling technology and precise energy delivery, COOLVIEW provides effective treatment for a wide range of vascular conditions whilst ensuring maximum patient comfort and safety.

How COOLVIEW Works

The system uses selective photothermolysis to target haemoglobin in blood vessels, causing controlled thermal damage to unwanted vessels whilst preserving surrounding tissue:

532nm KTP Wavelength

Ideal for superficial vessels, facial spider veins, and surface capillaries

1064nm Nd:YAG Wavelength

Penetrates deeper to treat larger vessels, leg veins, and deeper vascular lesions

Advanced Cooling

Integrated cooling system protects the epidermis and enhances patient comfort

Treatable Conditions

Facial Vascular Lesions

Spider Veins & Capillaries

Treats: Facial spider veins, broken capillaries, cherry angiomas

Wavelength: Primarily 532nm KTP for surface vessels

Sessions: 1-3 treatments typically required

Results: Immediate vessel closure with gradual fading

Rosacea & Redness

Diffuse Facial Redness

Treats: Rosacea, persistent facial redness, flushing

Approach: Gentle treatment of multiple small vessels

Sessions: 2-4 treatments for optimal results

Maintenance: Periodic treatments may be recommended

Leg Veins

Spider & Reticular Veins

Treats: Leg spider veins, small reticular veins

Wavelength: 1064nm Nd:YAG for deeper penetration

Sessions: 2-4 treatments depending on vessel size

Combination: May combine with sclerotherapy for larger veins

Treatment Process

Initial Assessment

Comprehensive vascular assessment to determine the most appropriate wavelength and treatment parameters for your specific condition.

Treatment Preparation

Treatment area is cleansed and protective eyewear provided. Cooling gel may be applied for additional comfort during treatment.

Laser Treatment

Precise laser pulses are delivered along the vessel pathway. You may feel a slight stinging sensation as each vessel is treated. Treatment duration varies from 15-60 minutes.

Post-Treatment Care

Cooling gel applied and aftercare instructions provided. Treated vessels may appear darker initially before gradually fading over the following weeks.

Post-Treatment Expectations

  • Treated vessels may appear darker or more prominent initially
  • Gradual fading occurs over 2-8 weeks as vessels are reabsorbed
  • Mild redness or swelling may occur for 24-48 hours
  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily and avoid sun exposure
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for 48 hours
  • Avoid hot baths, saunas, and vigorous exercise for 24 hours
  • For leg vein treatment, avoid tight clothing for 24 hours
  • Multiple sessions may be required for complete vessel clearance
