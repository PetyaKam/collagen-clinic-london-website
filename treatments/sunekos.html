What is Sunekos?

Sunekos is an FDA-approved injectable treatment that targets your skin's extracellular matrix (ECM), a complex network that supports and nourishes your skin cells. When the ECM is healthy, your skin looks and feels vibrant and youthful.

Unlike traditional fillers that simply fill lines, Sunekos focuses on improving the overall health and function of your skin. It combines hyaluronic acid to hydrate the skin with a special blend of amino acids, helping your skin 'remember' how to make vital proteins like collagen and elastin again, leading to more natural and long-lasting rejuvenation.

How Sunekos Works

Sunekos works with your body's natural processes rather than against them. The treatment delivers essential components to help your skin rebuild its extracellular matrix:

Hyaluronic Acid Hydration

Provides deep hydration and interacts with specific receptors to trigger cell signalling

Amino Acid Building Blocks

Acts as building blocks for collagen, elastin, and fibronectin production

Natural Regeneration

Stimulates gradual and controlled regeneration process without inflammation-related damage

Treatment Process

Pre-Treatment Consultation

Comprehensive assessment of skin concerns and goals. Customized treatment plan using Performa, 1200, or combination approach.

Treatment Sessions

Each session lasts 30-60 minutes. Series of 3-4 sessions spaced 14 days apart allows skin to gradually respond and build upon results.

Pain Management

Topical anaesthetic numbs treatment area beforehand. Most patients experience minimal discomfort during injections.

Immediate Recovery

Minimal downtime - return to normal activities immediately. Mild discomfort can be managed with over-the-counter pain relievers.

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications (aspirin) and certain supplements a few days before treatment
  • Refrain from alcohol consumption for 24 hours prior to procedure
  • Stay well-hydrated in the days leading up to treatment
  • Maintain healthy skincare routine to optimize skin condition
  • Discuss any medications or health conditions during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or applying makeup to treated areas for 4-6 hours
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive sun exposure for 24 hours
  • Continue to moisturise skin regularly and use sunscreen daily
  • Manage any mild discomfort with over-the-counter pain relievers if needed
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results
  • Maintain healthy lifestyle and skincare routine to support results

Side Effects & Safety

Common Side Effects

Safety Profile
