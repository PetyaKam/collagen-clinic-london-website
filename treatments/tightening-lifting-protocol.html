Tightening Protocol

Dr Zolman's Evidence-Based Protocol for treating sagging skin, volume loss, and facial contouring

Protocol Includes: 1 session of Sunekos Biostimulator Full Face & Eyes
1 session of Ultraformer HIFU Full Face (non-fat loss mode)
Target: Sagging skin, volume loss, facial contouring, skin tightening
Sessions Needed: 3 Sunekos Sessions (top up as needed), 1 or max 2 Ultraformer HIFU sessions (top up as needed)

What is the Tightening Protocol?

The Tightening Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address sagging skin, volume loss, and facial contouring concerns. The comprehensive protocol combines a few powerful treatments that work synergistically to address skin laxity from multiple angles.

Sunekos is an injectable biostimulator that combines amino acids and hyaluronic acid to regenerate the skin's Extracellular Matrix (ECM) from the inside out, stimulating the skin to naturally produce new collagen and elastin. It strengthens the skin barrier function and improves overall skin health, making skin more resilient to aging triggers, injected into the skin with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort.

Ultraformer HIFU in non-fat loss mode targets the deep SMAS layer to lift and tighten underlying facial structures, providing immediate and progressive lifting. This advanced HIFU technology delivers precise deep tissue treatment for natural-looking results.

Who Can Benefit from the Tightening Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various types of sagging and volume loss:

Sagging Skin

Those with loose, sagging skin on the face, neck, and jawline

Volume Loss

Individuals with hollowed cheeks, temples, or other areas of facial volume loss

Facial Contouring

Those seeking to improve facial contours and achieve a more defined jawline

Preventive Care

Anyone seeking to prevent future sagging and maintain youthful facial contours
