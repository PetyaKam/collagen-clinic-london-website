What is the Tightening & Lifting Protocol?

The Tightening & Lifting Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address sagging skin, volume loss, and facial contouring concerns. This comprehensive protocol combines the collagen-stimulating benefits of Sculptra with the deep-tissue tightening of HIFU and the surface-level improvements of EXION for maximum lifting and contouring results.

This protocol targets sagging at multiple levels - from the deep SMAS layer to the surface skin - while restoring volume and improving overall facial contours for a more youthful appearance.

How the Tightening & Lifting Protocol Works

The protocol combines three powerful treatments that work synergistically to address sagging and volume loss from multiple angles:

Sculptra Treatment

Stimulates natural collagen production to restore volume and improve facial contours, providing long-lasting structural support

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound)

Targets the deep SMAS layer to lift and tighten underlying facial structures, providing immediate and progressive lifting

EXION Radiofrequency

Improves skin texture and provides additional tightening at the surface level, enhancing overall skin quality

Treatment Process

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive facial analysis to assess sagging, volume loss, and contouring goals. Customised treatment plan development.

Treatment Sessions

3-4 sessions spaced 3-4 weeks apart. Each session includes Sculptra injections, HIFU treatment, and EXION radiofrequency.

Session Duration

Each treatment session lasts approximately 90-120 minutes, including preparation, treatment, and recovery time.

Follow-up Care

Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and maintain results with additional treatments as needed.

Expected Results

Immediate Effects

Some improvement in skin texture and hydration may be visible immediately after treatment

Weeks 2-4

Gradual improvement in skin texture and initial lifting effects from HIFU treatment

Weeks 4-8

Significant lifting and contouring improvements as Sculptra stimulates collagen production

Long-term Benefits

Continued improvement over 6-12 months with maintained results and enhanced facial contours

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid sun exposure and use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily for 2 weeks before treatment
  • Discontinue use of retinoids and other active skincare products 1 week prior
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before treatment
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Discuss any medications, health conditions, or recent cosmetic procedures during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or applying makeup to treated areas for 4-6 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive sun exposure for 24-48 hours
  • Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Massage treated areas as directed by your practitioner to ensure optimal Sculptra distribution
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results

Side Effects & Safety

All components of the Tightening & Lifting Protocol have excellent safety profiles when administered by qualified practitioners:

Common Side Effects

Mild redness, swelling, or bruising at injection sites; temporary tenderness from HIFU treatment

Recovery Time

Minimal downtime - can usually resume normal activities immediately following treatment

Safety Profile

All treatments are FDA-approved with excellent safety records when performed by qualified practitioners
