What is the Redness Protocol?

The Redness Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address facial redness, rosacea, and vascular concerns. This comprehensive protocol combines the skin-revitalising benefits of Sunekos with the precision of Genesis laser technology to target the underlying causes of redness and improve overall skin health.

Unlike traditional treatments that may only address surface symptoms, this protocol works at the cellular level to strengthen skin barrier function, reduce inflammation, and improve vascular health for long-lasting results.

How the Redness Protocol Works

The protocol combines two powerful treatments that work synergistically to address redness from multiple angles:

Sunekos Treatment

Strengthens skin barrier function and reduces inflammation at the cellular level, helping skin become more resilient to triggers

Genesis Laser

Targets visible blood vessels and redness with precision, while stimulating collagen production for improved skin texture

Combined Benefits

Addresses both the underlying causes and visible symptoms of redness for comprehensive treatment

Treatment Process

Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and maintain results with additional treatments as needed.

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before treatment
  • Discontinue use of retinoids and other active skincare products 3 days prior
  • Avoid sun exposure and use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily
  • Stay well-hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine
  • Discuss any medications or health conditions during consultation

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching or applying makeup to treated areas for 4-6 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products for the first week
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive sun exposure for 24-48 hours
  • Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen daily and avoid direct sun exposure
  • Use cool compresses if experiencing mild redness or swelling
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled for optimal results

Side Effects & Safety

Safety Profile
