What is the Redness Protocol?

The Redness Protocol is Dr Zolman's evidence-based treatment approach specifically designed to address facial redness, rosacea, and vascular concerns. The comprehensive protocol combines a few powerful treatments that work synergistically to address redness from multiple angles.

Sunekos is an injectable biostimulator that combines amino acids and hyaluronic acid to regenerate the skin's Extracellular Matrix (ECM) from the inside out, stimulating the skin to naturally produce new collagen and elastin. It strengthens the skin barrier function and improves overall skin health, making skin more resilient to pigmentation triggers, injected into the skin with the world's smallest needles for minimal discomfort.

Excel V Genesis Laser and Excel V CoolView Laser work together to target visible blood vessels and redness with precision. These advanced vascular lasers stimulate collagen production while addressing broken capillaries and vascular concerns for comprehensive treatment of facial redness and rosacea.

Who Can Benefit from the Redness Protocol?

This protocol is ideal for individuals experiencing various forms of facial redness and vascular concerns:

Rosacea Sufferers

Those with persistent facial redness, flushing, and sensitivity associated with rosacea

Broken Capillaries

Individuals with visible broken blood vessels or spider veins on the face

General Redness

Anyone experiencing persistent facial redness or uneven skin tone

Sensitive Skin

Those with reactive skin that flushes easily or becomes irritated
