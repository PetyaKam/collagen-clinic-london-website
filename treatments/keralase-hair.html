What is KeraLase Hair Treatment?

KeraLase is an advanced laser hair regeneration treatment that combines low-level laser therapy (LLLT) with specialized scalp treatments to stimulate hair follicles, improve scalp circulation, and promote natural hair regrowth.

This innovative treatment targets the root causes of hair loss and thinning, including poor circulation, inflammation, and weakened hair follicles, providing a non-invasive solution for both men and women experiencing various types of hair loss.

How KeraLase Works

The KeraLase system uses advanced laser technology combined with specialized scalp treatments to:

Stimulate Hair Follicles

Low-level laser therapy activates dormant hair follicles and promotes new hair growth

Improve Scalp Circulation

Enhanced blood flow delivers essential nutrients to hair follicles for optimal growth

Reduce Inflammation

Laser therapy helps reduce scalp inflammation that can contribute to hair loss

Treatment Process

Pre-Treatment Consultation

Comprehensive scalp analysis and hair loss assessment. Treatment plan customized based on individual hair loss pattern and goals.

Treatment Day

Scalp is cleansed and prepared. Laser therapy is applied systematically across the treatment area. Treatment typically takes 20-30 minutes.

Immediate Effects

Improved scalp circulation and reduced inflammation. No downtime - return to normal activities immediately.

Progressive Results

Hair regrowth becomes visible after 4-8 weeks, with optimal results achieved after completing the full treatment series.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos for the first week
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments for 48 hours
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated to support hair follicle health
  • Use recommended hair care products to support growth
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and tight hairstyles
  • Follow up appointments as scheduled
  • Be patient - hair regrowth takes time and consistency
