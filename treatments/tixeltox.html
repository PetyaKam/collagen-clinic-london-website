What is TixelTox?

TixelTox is an innovative combination treatment that utilises TIXEL fractional technology to create microscopic channels in the skin, followed by the application of botulinum toxin. This revolutionary approach enhances the penetration and distribution of botulinum toxin for superior aesthetic outcomes.
